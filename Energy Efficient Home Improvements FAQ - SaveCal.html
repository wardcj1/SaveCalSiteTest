What services do you offer?

We offer a variety of energy efficient upgrades to your home or commercial property, these include:

  • Energy efficient “Cool roofs”
  • Energy efficient central heating/air
  • Energy efficient windows
  • Energy efficient exterior paint
  • Solar panels

What kind of credit score do I need to get approved?

All credit scores are welcome. Our approval process is based on the equity you have in your home, and not is not based on your credit score.
